1) Select a Survival Kit from the languages presented.

2) Read through all sections of the Pronunciation Guide to familiarize yourself with the romanization of the language.

3) Select any of the modules in the Basic Survival Kit. There is no recommended order, however, "Helpful Words, Phrases & Questions" and "Greetings & Introductions" are a good place to start.

4) Read the English. Then click the to play the audio for each item. Play the audio once more while silently reading the romanized version of the word or phrase. Now, repeat the phrase aloud in the foreign language while reading the romanized form.

5) Navigate between pages by clicking the icons on the top and bottom right of each page.

6) Print an entire module by click the Print button.

7) When you have completed a module, click on the menu button to return to the main menu.

If you have any questions, comments, suggestions, or if you experience problems, please contact the Instructional Design Department, DLIFLC, by clicking the "Provide Feedback" button below.
