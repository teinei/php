The Online Language Survival Kits are developed for a Microsoft PC Operating Environment using either Internet Explorer 6.0 / Netscape 7.0 or later version browser. The kits designed to display at screen resolutions of 800x600 or higher.

Depending on the language, specific support may be required. Language support kits can normally be downloaded from the Microsoft support site. Additionally, some languages were not well supported prior to Windows 2000/XP (Arabic, Persian as well as other right-to-left languages).

Audio playback is achieved using the FLASH 7.0+ plug-in. If audio does not play, download the latest FLASH plug-in from Adobe Download Page
